About Cooper:

Cooper Pflaum is a noted Austrian furry, arsonist, and duelist of Canadian descent. He was born at the age of six without a face, but stole one from a different six year old by the age of seven. His body has since aged, but his face has not.

Pflaum studies cybersecurity as the University of Cincinnati in hopes of one day hacking area 51 to "Release the kraken". Notably his ex boyfriend was already going to do this before Pflaum killed him in a duel.

Notable facts and figures:

  • Pflaum dated the president of furry club for six years before killing him in a duel.
  • Cooper hates the other members of lab, especially J. Carver whom he killed in a duel.
  • He is the head of lab at Cyber@UC since he usurped the position from Anthony Arcuri, whom Pflaum killed in a duel.
  • Pflaum once brought a knife to a gun fight by accident but won by sacrificing a third of his brain. He has not been able to taste butter since.
